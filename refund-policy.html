Refund Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

This Refund Policy explains when payments for Stromatic Media services (“Services”) may be refunded. By placing an order, you acknowledge this policy.

1. All sales are final after brief submission

Our productized Services are prepared and scheduled immediately after checkout. Once you have submitted your brief and completed payment, all sales are final except as expressly stated in Section 2 below.

2. Limited exceptions

We may issue a refund or credit, at our sole discretion, if:

  • We are unable to deliver the stated deliverable due to an operational failure on our side and cannot cure within a reasonable time;
  • A duplicate charge or clear payment-processing error occurred;
  • We declined or cancelled the order before work began, in which case you will not be charged, or any charge will be reversed.

Refunds are not available for change of mind, dissatisfaction with creative direction where the work matches the stated scope, delays caused by incomplete client input, or force majeure events.

3. Mentor sessions

If you miss a scheduled session without reasonable notice, fees may be forfeited. Rescheduling is handled case-by-case by email.

4. How to request help

Email stromaticmedia@gmail.com with your order details and a clear description of the issue. We aim to respond within a few business days.

5. Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback without first contacting us may slow resolution. We will provide evidence of delivery to payment processors where applicable.

6. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date reflects the current version.
